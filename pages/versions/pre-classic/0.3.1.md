## Optimization
- We brought back the greedy meshing (someting i personally dont like but is severly needed)
- Updated Face culling to the generation (so faces not visible gets ignored)
- chunk messing (rework)
- mesh caching
- frustum culling (a hella larger update to it)
- occlusion culling (if something blocks the view of another chunk that chunk doesnt get loaded)
- ambient occlusion Baking (Makes he lighting better)
- VBO  update
- Mesh updates queues (less lag spikes)
- Block Update Batching (rebuilds mesh instead of block)
- Light Propagation Optimization (light comes in queues instead of all in the same time)
- Sparse Chunk Storage (useless chunks like air is not stored)
- Heightmap Caching (stores the highest block in every column)
- Entity Activation Range (entitys far away is frozen not despawned, and enitys outside of the render distance get despawned)
- Tick Limiting (water and so is limited to ticks now)
